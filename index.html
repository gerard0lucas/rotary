Why Now? Why Bangalore Lepakshi?

Bangalore is a city of innovation and growth, and with that comes a unique set of community needs and opportunities. The Rotary Club of Bangalore Lepakshi is being formed to specifically address these, creating targeted, effective service projects that resonate with the heart of our vibrant city.

Educating Tomorrow's Leaders

Supporting local schools and literacy programs

Healthy Communities

Driving health awareness and medical camps

Sustainable Bangalore

Championing environmental initiatives

Empowering Entrepreneurs

Fostering local economic growth

Who We Are

The Rotary Club of Bangalore Lepakshi is in its formative stages, set to become a dynamic new chapter of Rotary International in Bangalore. We are currently inviting passionate individuals to join us as charter members – those who will lay the foundation for a club dedicated to impactful service, strong fellowship, and ethical leadership.

We envision a club that embodies the innovative spirit of Bangalore, channeling our collective energy and expertise into projects that bring tangible, positive change to our local communities and connect us to a global network of problem-solvers.

Our Vision

To establish a vibrant, impactful Rotary Club in Bangalore Lepakshi, renowned for its sustainable service projects, strong member engagement, and significant contributions to peace and community well-being.

Our Mission

To unite emerging and established leaders in Bangalore to found a club dedicated to humanitarian service, promote high ethical standards, and advance goodwill and understanding, starting with our local community and extending globally.
